FACILITIES

We all have encountered the struggle of our parents and grandparents for education. Earlier the schools were far from home or under a tree with thatched roofs. All those stories are of the past.Today, the classrooms, infrastructure, everything has changed and assessed asa basic necessity for education. Campus facilities are important for the educational sector and play a pivotal role in improving the work efficiency of students and teachers.We offer the following facilities and many more.

World Class Library

It is the place where student expose themselves to great knowledge. We have a collection of 10,000 books+ in our library.

Largest Play Ground

As the metal and physical health is also most important for students we have a very large palysround for them.

Tasty and Healthy Food

We have a menu of very tasty items. Students here say that they enjoy this delicious food.

Our Gym

A state of art, Gymnasium nourishes several enthusiasts to develop their fitness & sports activities.Students can avail the facilities of gym with the proper guidance of Physical Trainer.

HOSTEL

Separate hostel accommodation is provided for boys and girls. There is one large hostel for boys and one hostel for girls. All the hostels have common rooms, 24 hour Wifi-connectivity and recreational centres where newspapers, magazines and TVs are available. Round the clock water and electricity is provided. Laundry equipments are also available in the hostels.

LABS

Eduford University contains different labs of different courses with an efficient equipment and infrastructure.

CLASS ROOMS

The class rooms are pretty good which provides students with a lot of comfort to listen the classes.

SEMINAR HALL

It contains different seminar halls for different courses.
